Activities

Participant of SuperAI Engineer Season2, 2021 - AIAT

SuperAI Engineer is a program run by the Artificial Intelligence Association of Thailand (AIAT), which is dedicated to developing artificial intelligence innovators, engineers, researchers, and startups. And I'm one of the applicants who advanced to the second round, or level two, which includes a more challenging course and work.

Backend Developer at Hightech club - Chulalongkorn University

Hightech Club is a Chulalongkorn club whose mission is to recruit students with a passion for web development and to improve members' web development skills, such as backend, frontend, marketing, and UXUI skills. I'm a backend web developer who works with NodeJs, Express, and Prisma on the server side. In order to construct web applications, I also combine my frontend skills and all basic knowledges I have learnt.

Strategy team of Chula Legal Tech Year2, 2021 - Faculty of Law's Chulalongkorn University and Tilleke & Gibbins international Ltd.

Chula Legal Tech is a competition for Innovation and Technology to develop the law industry. I created and implemented a plan to eliminate unauthorized parking and traffic offenses. Our Balloon Tech team was one of twenty that qualified.

Frontend Developer at Thinc 10 days miracle project - Chulalongkorn University

I was a member of a Thinc club whose mission was to recruit students with a passion for web development and to improve members' web development abilities, such as backend skills, frontend skills, project management skills, and UXUI skills. I learned all of the fundamentals of frontend development, including HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Bootstrap. With the help of a teammate, I finished the task of building a given web project in ten days. Taking compliments from judges who were former members of the club and are now employees or owners of major corporations.
